Frequently Asked

Pricing Questions

Are there any hidden fees? +

Never. Our quotes include all standard fees. Gratuity (15–20%) and applicable tolls are the only additions you may see. We always tell you exactly what your total will be before confirming your booking.

Do prices include tolls and parking? +

Tolls for the standard route are included in airport transfers (noted above). Any unusual toll or parking charges incurred at your request will be listed separately on your invoice — never a surprise.

What is the cancellation policy? +

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 12–24 hours: 50% charge. Less than 12 hours or no-shows: full charge. We understand plans change — contact us as early as possible.

Do you offer corporate account rates? +

Yes. Corporate accounts benefit from negotiated rates, consolidated monthly invoicing, dedicated account management, and priority vehicle allocation. Contact our team to set up your corporate account today.

How is gratuity handled? +

Gratuity is not included in quoted prices. We suggest 15–20% of the fare as a standard for excellent service. You may add it to your payment during booking or provide it to your chauffeur directly.

Can I get a custom quote for a large event? +

Absolutely. Large events, multi-vehicle coordination, and full-day arrangements receive custom pricing. Submit details via our booking form or call us directly and we will provide a comprehensive event quote within 2 hours.
